The 36 Channels

Channels are the pathways that connect Centers and create definition in the Bodygraph. When both gates of a channel are activated, a fixed energy flow is established, shaping who you are and how your energy operates in the world.

| What Are Channels?

A channel is a pathway that connects two Centers in the Bodygraph. Each channel is formed by two Gates, one at each end. There are 36 channels in total, and together they form the circuitry of the Human Design system.

When both gates of a channel are activated in your chart, whether by your conscious (Personality) or unconscious (Design) calculations, the channel is defined. A defined channel creates a fixed, reliable energy flow between the two Centers it connects, and both of those Centers become defined (colored in) as a result. This is how definition is created in a chart.

If only one gate of a channel is activated, it is called a hanging gate. The channel remains undefined, and the energy is incomplete. Hanging gates often create an attraction to others who carry the missing gate. When two people each bring one gate of the same channel, they form an electromagnetic connection that temporarily completes the circuit between them.

Channels are grouped into circuits, families of channels that share a common theme. The three main circuit groups are Individual (mutation and empowerment), Collective (sharing through logic and experience), and Tribal (support, resources, and community). A small group of Integration channels stands apart, focused purely on personal survival and self-empowerment.

Each defined channel in your chart represents a consistent life theme, a specific energy that you can rely on and that others experience in you. Your channels are not something you do; they are something you are. Understanding them is key to understanding your definition.

| Frequently Asked Questions

What is a channel in Human Design?
A channel is a pathway that connects two Centers in the Bodygraph. Each channel is formed by two gates, one at each end. When both gates are activated in your chart, the channel is defined and creates a fixed, reliable energy flow between those two Centers. There are 36 channels in total, and they are the mechanism by which definition is created. Your defined channels represent consistent life themes and energy that you can always rely on.
What happens when only one gate of a channel is defined?
When only one gate of a channel is activated in your chart, the channel remains undefined and no energy flows between the two Centers. This single activated gate is called a hanging gate. Hanging gates create a magnetic pull toward others who carry the missing gate. When two people each bring one gate of the same channel, they complete the circuit electromagnetically. This is one of the most powerful dynamics in relationship mechanics.
What is the difference between a circuit and a channel?
A channel is a single pathway connecting two Centers. A circuit is a group of channels that share a common theme and function. The three main circuit groups are Individual (mutation and empowerment), Collective (sharing through logic and experience), and Tribal (support, resources, and community). Integration channels stand apart as they focus purely on self-empowerment and survival. Understanding which circuits your channels belong to reveals the broader themes operating in your design.
Can transits define a channel temporarily?
Yes. When a planetary transit activates a gate that completes a channel with one of your natal gates, that channel becomes temporarily defined. This transit connection can temporarily define Centers and shift how your chart is experienced in the moment. This is why you may feel different on certain days. Your natal definition remains fixed, but the transit field creates a constantly changing overlay.
How do channels relate to definition in a chart?
Channels are the mechanism that creates definition. When a channel is defined, both Centers it connects become defined (colored in on the Bodygraph). Your defined channels help create the pattern of definition in your chart, including which Centers are defined, how your definition is structured, and the fixed energy themes that operate consistently in your life. Definition is what makes you reliable. It is the energy that is always there.
