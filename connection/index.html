The Connection Chart

When two people come together, a new design emerges. The Connection Chart reveals the mechanics of a relationship, showing what is created between two individuals that neither possesses alone.

| How the Connection Chart Works

In Human Design, a Connection Chart (also called a Composite Chart) is created by overlaying two individual Bodygraphs. When both charts are combined, new channels may be completed, new Centers may become defined, and an entirely new energetic configuration emerges. This composite design represents the mechanics of the relationship itself.

The Connection Chart is not about compatibility in the traditional sense. It reveals the specific dynamics that arise when two people share the same space. Some connections create definition that amplifies energy. Others create electromagnetic attractions. Still others introduce conditioning that can feel challenging or unfamiliar. The chart makes these dynamics visible and measurable.

Dominance

Shared Definition

Dominance occurs when both people have the same channel defined in their individual charts. This creates a shared, fixed energy between them. The channel is always present in the connection, and both people experience it as a reliable, consistent force. Dominance themes often feel natural and require little negotiation.

Compromise

One-Sided Definition

Compromise occurs when one person has a full channel defined and the other does not. The person with the channel brings a fixed energy into the relationship, while the other receives it as conditioning. This dynamic requires awareness because the conditioned person may take on energy that is not naturally theirs.

Electromagnetic Connection

Attraction Through Polarity

An electromagnetic connection forms when one person has one Gate of a channel and the other person has the opposite Gate, completing the channel between them. This is the classic mechanism of attraction in Human Design. The completed channel creates a new energy that neither person has alone, generating a pull that can feel magnetic and compelling.

Companionship

Shared Gates Without a Channel

Companionship occurs when both people have the same Gate activated but without completing a channel. This creates a sense of familiarity and mutual understanding around a shared theme. The energy resonates between them without creating new definition.

Defined Centers in the Connection

New Definition Between Two People

When two charts combine and new channels are completed, previously undefined Centers can become defined in the Connection Chart. This means the relationship creates consistent, reliable energy that neither individual carries on their own. This new definition shapes the character and dynamics of the relationship.

Open Centers in the Connection

Shared Vulnerability

Centers that remain undefined in the Connection Chart represent areas where both people are open and vulnerable together. These are the places where the relationship is most susceptible to outside conditioning. They are also the areas where the deepest learning and wisdom can develop over time.

The Connection as Its Own Entity

More Than the Sum of Parts

A Connection Chart is not simply two charts added together. It represents a distinct energetic entity with its own Type, definition pattern, and dynamics. The relationship has its own mechanical signature that operates independently of either individual. This is why some relationships feel profoundly different from what either person experiences alone.

Practical Use

Awareness in Relationships

The Connection Chart is a tool for awareness, not judgment. It does not determine whether a relationship will succeed or fail. It reveals the specific mechanical dynamics at play so that both people can navigate the connection with greater understanding. This applies to romantic partnerships, friendships, business relationships, and family dynamics.

Note

The Connection Chart reveals the mechanics of the relationship, not its quality. A chart full of electromagnetic connections is not inherently better or worse than one with dominance or compromise. What matters is awareness of the dynamics and how each person navigates them through their own Strategy and Authority.

| Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Connection Chart in Human Design?

A Connection Chart is created by overlaying two individual Bodygraphs to reveal the mechanics of a relationship. When both charts are combined, new channels may form, new Centers may become defined, and a unique energetic configuration emerges. This composite design shows the specific dynamics that exist between two people.

What is an electromagnetic connection?

An electromagnetic connection occurs when one person has one Gate of a channel and the other person has the opposite Gate, completing the channel between them. This creates a new energy that neither person has individually and is one of the primary mechanisms of attraction in Human Design. The pull can feel magnetic and compelling.

What is the difference between dominance and compromise?

Dominance occurs when both people have the same channel fully defined in their individual charts, creating a shared fixed energy. Compromise occurs when only one person has the channel defined, bringing a fixed energy that conditions the other person. Dominance tends to feel natural and consistent, while compromise requires awareness of the conditioning dynamic.

Can a Connection Chart predict relationship success?

No. The Connection Chart reveals the mechanical dynamics between two people, but it does not predict outcomes. A relationship with many electromagnetic connections is not inherently better than one with dominance or compromise. What determines the quality of a relationship is how each person navigates the connection through their own Strategy and Authority.
