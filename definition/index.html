Foundation · Definition

Definition

Definition describes how your defined Centers are connected - or separated. It shapes how you process information, experience relationships, and where your deepest conditioning lives.

| Tools, not doctrine.

The Human Design System was transmitted through Ra Uru Hu beginning in 1987. Genetic Matrix provides these tools for your own exploration and experiment. Definition is a mechanical concept - it describes energy flow, not personality. Understanding your Definition supports your experiment with Strategy and Authority but does not replace it.

| What Is Definition?

When two Centers in your bodygraph are connected by a complete Channel (both Gates activated), they form a definition - a fixed, reliable flow of energy between them. Definition is the colored-in parts of your chart: the defined Centers and the Channels that connect them.

Definition type describes how these defined areas relate to each other. Are they all connected in one circuit? Or are they separated into two, three, or four independent groups? This seemingly simple distinction has profound effects on how you process information, relate to others, and experience conditioning.

There are five possible Definition types: Single, Split (Small and Large), Triple Split, Quadruple Split, and No Definition (Reflectors only). Each creates a fundamentally different experience of life.

Definition is not about intelligence or capability - it is about how your energy flows internally. A Quadruple Split is not "slower" in ability than a Single Definition. They simply need more time for their complex internal circuitry to integrate information before acting.

| Processing Speed

One of the most practical effects of Definition is how quickly you process information. As you move from Single to Split to Triple Split to Quadruple Split, everything slows down. A Single Definition integrates information the moment it receives it. A Quadruple Split needs the most time of all.

| Conditioning and Bridging

Conditioned by Open Centers

Single Definition and Triple Split are primarily conditioned through their undefined Centers. The open Centers are where the mind pulls them away from their authentic nature. For these types, knowing the not-self questions of their undefined Centers is the most important awareness practice.

Conditioned by Bridging Gates

Split Definition and Quadruple Split are primarily conditioned through the bridging gates between their defined areas. They are magnetically drawn to whatever fills the gap, and this attraction can override Strategy and Authority. Knowing which specific gates bridge the splits is essential.

| The Definition Types

Each Definition type creates a fundamentally different way of processing, relating, and experiencing conditioning. Understanding yours is one of the most practical pieces of your chart.

| Definition and Relationships

Your Definition type fundamentally shapes how you experience relationships and what you are drawn to in others. This is one of the most practical applications of understanding your Definition.

Single

Does not seek completion through others. Self-contained and internally whole. Relationships are not about filling a gap - they are about sharing and resonance.

Split

Drawn to people who bridge the gap between the two halves. The classic "you complete me" dynamic. The most relationship-oriented Definition type.

Triple

Needs variety and multiple auras rather than one bridging person. Does best in social environments, teams, and groups where different people activate different areas.

Quadruple

Needs a small curated circle of two or three specific people who together bridge all four areas. Not one person, not the crowd - a carefully selected inner circle.

| Common Questions

What is Definition in Human Design?
Definition describes how your defined Centers are connected or separated. It determines how quickly you process information, how you experience relationships, and where your primary conditioning occurs. There are four types plus No Definition for Reflectors.
What is the difference between Small Split and Large Split?
A Small Split has a narrow gap - just one missing gate. The person experiences it as "my problem" and is drawn to the specific missing gate. A Large Split has a wider gap - multiple gates or a whole Channel - and the person tends to see it as "their problem," projecting the disconnection outward.
How does Definition affect processing speed?
Single Definition processes instantly. Split Definition is slower - information waits for the bridge. Triple Split is slower still. Quadruple Split is the slowest. This is not intelligence - it is integration time. Emotional Authority adds additional time on top.
How does Definition affect relationships?
Single Definition does not seek completion. Split Definition is drawn to people who bridge the gap. Triple Split needs variety and multiple auras. Quadruple Split needs a small curated circle of two or three specific people.
Where does conditioning happen?
For Single and Triple Split, conditioning comes primarily through undefined Centers. For Split and Quadruple Split, conditioning is concentrated in the bridging gates between defined areas. Both dynamics exist in all types, but the emphasis differs.
What does No Definition mean?
No Definition is exclusive to Reflectors - people with no defined Centers. Without fixed energy, they take in and amplify everything from their environment. They are designed to sample and reflect the world around them.
Can Definition change?
No - your Definition is fixed at birth. However, transits temporarily bridge splits, and the people around you constantly activate different bridging configurations. Your experience of your Definition shifts based on environment and company.
