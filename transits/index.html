Transits in Human Design

The neutrino stream carries a constant flow of planetary programming that conditions everyone in realtime. Learn how transiting planets activate Gates and shape the collective field.

| How Transits Work

In Human Design, transits represent the current planetary positions and the Gates they activate in realtime. The neutrino stream, a flow of subatomic particles passing through everything, carries the imprint of each planet's position. This creates a constantly shifting field of conditioning that affects everyone on the planet, regardless of their individual design.

Transits do not change your chart. Your natal design is fixed. Instead, transits create temporary activations that overlay your chart, potentially defining Centers, creating channels, and activating Gates that are not part of your birth configuration. Understanding transits helps you recognize when external conditioning is influencing your experience.

The Transit Field

The Current Planetary Program

The transit field is the set of planetary positions at any given moment. Just as your birth chart captures a snapshot of the planetary gates at the time you were born, the transit field captures what is happening right now. Every planet in the transit field activates a specific Gate and Line, creating a collective program that conditions everyone simultaneously.

How Transits Condition Your Chart

Temporary Definition

When a transiting planet activates a Gate that connects to one of your natal Gates, it can temporarily create a channel and define Centers that are normally open or undefined in your design. This temporary definition brings experiences and pressures that are not naturally part of your makeup. Recognizing transit-created definition helps you distinguish between what is yours and what is conditioning.

Sun Transits

The Dominant Daily Theme

The Sun moves through each Gate approximately every 5.7 days. Because the Sun accounts for a large share of the neutrino stream, its transit position sets the dominant collective theme at any given time. When the transiting Sun activates a Gate in your chart, that theme becomes particularly prominent in your experience.

Moon Transits

Emotional and Motivational Shifts

The Moon transits through each Gate roughly every 10–11 hours, creating rapid shifts in the collective emotional and motivational backdrop. Moon transits are subtle but frequent, often experienced as brief mood shifts or changes in drive.

Planetary Transit Speeds

From Hours to Decades

Each planet moves through the Gates at a different speed. The Moon changes Gates every few hours. The Sun takes about 5.7 days per Gate. Mercury, Venus, and Mars move through Gates over days to weeks. The outer planets (Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune, Pluto) can stay in a single Gate for months or even years, creating sustained background conditioning.

Global Cycles and Collective Themes

Generational Conditioning

The slow-moving outer planets create long-term collective themes. When Pluto, Neptune, or Uranus transit through a particular Gate, that theme permeates the collective consciousness for extended periods. These transits shape generational experiences and cultural shifts visible across entire populations.

Transit Channels

Collective Definition in Realtime

When two transiting planets form a complete channel (activating both Gates of a channel simultaneously), that channel becomes defined in the global transit field. This creates a collective experience that everyone feels, particularly those with open Centers connected by the transit channel.

Using Transits Practically

Awareness, Not Prediction

Transits are not meant for prediction or fortune-telling. In Human Design, their value lies in awareness. By understanding what the transit field is activating, you can notice when your behavior, mood, or decisions are being influenced by external conditioning rather than your own design. This awareness supports your experiment with Strategy and Authority.

Note

Transits are the conditioning field that affects everyone. They do not override your design. Your Strategy and Authority remain your primary navigation tools regardless of what the transit field is doing.
